Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with thinly sliced meat, vegetables, and a variety of spices. This recipe is made with marinated chicken, fresh vegetables, and a creamy tahini sauce. It's a flavorful and satisfying dish that is perfect for a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Pita bread, for serving
  • Sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onions, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add chicken thighs to the bowl and toss to coat in the marinade.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  4. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Grill chicken thighs for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, whisk together yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, and parsley in a small bowl.
  7. Remove chicken from grill and let rest for a few minutes before slicing thinly.
  8. Warm pita bread on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  9. Assemble shawarma by placing sliced chicken, cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onions on pita bread.
  10. Drizzle with tahini sauce and serve immediately.
