Curry

Description

Curry is a popular dish in Indian cuisine made with a variety of spices and ingredients. This recipe is made with chicken, tomatoes, and a blend of spices. It's a flavorful and aromatic dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add onion and garlic to skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, yogurt, curry powder, paprika, cumin, garam masala, salt, and pepper.
  6. Return chicken to skillet and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  8. Serve over rice or with naan bread.
